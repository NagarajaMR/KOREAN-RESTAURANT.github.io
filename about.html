About Me

In 1990 Byung-ho (병호) started first restaurants in Seoul , now called 和え魚コレア. The other prominent chain of 和え魚コレア is the 和え魚コレア group, founded by Dal (달), who gave up a low-grade salaried position inSeoul to join his brothers' snack food (tiffin) business inSeoul in 1995.

MISSION
